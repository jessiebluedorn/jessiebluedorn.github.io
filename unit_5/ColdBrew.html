Have a Cold Brew Coffee in NYC

  1. Birch

    2. 21 E 27th St, New York, NY 10016

    I love how spacious this location is-- it's much larger than some of their other mahattan spots.


  2. Ludlow Coffee Supply

    3. 176 Ludlow St, New York, NY 10002

    A fun hipster choice in the LES, this coffee shop is located right next to the Ludlow Hotel and actually has a working barbershop in the back. I'm a big fan of their plants.


  3. Perk Kafe

    4. 162 E 37th St, New York, NY 10016

    Perk is best described as cozy, which is a nice change of pace from some of the very trendy options on this list.


  4. Stumptown Coffee Roasters

    5. Ace Hotel New York, 18 W 29th St, New York, NY 10001

    Located in the lobby of the Ace Hotel, Stumptown is a great choice if you want to post up in the Ace Lobby (which doubles as a communal workspace) and fire off some eamails.
